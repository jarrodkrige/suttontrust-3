Here’s a strong **editorial + friendly** font pairing that works well for a charity microsite aimed at sixth‑formers:

### **Display font: Playfair Display**

### **Body font: Inter**

**Why this pairing works:**

*   **Playfair Display (headings)** – Brings an editorial, almost magazine-like feel that signals credibility and storytelling. Its high-contrast serif style feels expressive and human, helping the charity come across as thoughtful and authentic rather than corporate.
*   **Inter (body)** – Clean, modern, and highly readable on screens, especially for longer content. It keeps the site feeling current and accessible for younger audiences without clashing with the more expressive display font.

**Overall effect:**  
Playfair gives you warmth, personality, and a sense of purpose, while Inter keeps everything easy to read and digitally native—together striking a balance between **serious (trustworthy charity) and friendly (youth-focused, approachable).**

You are redesigning the hero section of a first‑generation university support website called “FirstGen”.

Your goal is to make the homepage feel welcoming, reassuring, and clear — especially for students who may feel uncertain, out of place, or unsure how university works.

Instructions:
- Replace generic headline text with warm, confidence‑building language.
- Avoid corporate, academic, or marketing jargon.
- Speak directly to the student in plain English.
- Emphasise that it’s okay to feel unsure — support is available.
- Use a tone that is encouraging, calm, and human.
- Keep the layout clean and spacious.
- Add a large, friendly hero image showing students sitting together on grass in a relaxed, inclusive university setting.
- Ensure primary CTA stands out (“Get started”).
- This is not a recruitment site — it is a support and guidance space.

The user interface should feel modern, inclusive, and trustworthy.

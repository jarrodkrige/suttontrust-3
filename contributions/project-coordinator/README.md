# 📋 Project Coordinator

You're the team's friendly face for the hour. Help the room feel good, keep
the conversation moving, and notice when someone's quiet so they don't get
left behind.

Everyone on the team can commit to the repo — your job isn't to direct, it's
to make people feel welcome.

## What lives in this folder — pick whichever calls to you

- `team-plan.md` — a few notes on what the site might be (sections, tone,
  call to action). Optional sketch — the team will riff on it.
- `flow-notes.md` — your sense of the page's order ("voices before numbers
  feels warmer"). The Factory Agent treats this as the flow source of truth.
- `status-updates.md` — short notes as the hour goes ("the designers found
  their style", "we have a hero image now")
- `blockers.md` — anything blocking a teammate that the Product Owner could
  unblock

You don't have to commit all of these — pick what feels useful and leave
the rest. Even a short paragraph in `team-plan.md` is gold for the agent.

## Coordinate with

- **Pitch Lead(s)** — they shape the team's story; chat about what's
  emerging
- **Software Developers** — they shape the page; your `flow-notes.md` is
  authoritative on section order
- **Everyone** — celebrate the first commit from each teammate

## First 2 minutes

1. Sign in to **copilot.microsoft.com** with your role-card account.
2. Try one of the prompts below.
3. Drop a few notes in `team-plan.md` — even one paragraph is enough.
4. Commit. Then go say hi to a teammate.

## Prompts that work

- *"What 4 things might a charity site for UK sixth-formers want to cover?"*
- *"Suggest 3 friendly questions I could ask a teammate to help them get started."*
- *"Draft a warm welcome line for first-gen students reading this site for the first time."*
- *"What would make this team feel proud at the end of an hour together?"*
